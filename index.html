Film Making

Film is considered to be an important art form, a source of popular entertainment, and a powerful medium for indoctrinating citizens. The visual basis of film gives it a universal power of communication.

Portforlio

The portfolio is also known as the film reel or director’s showreel. More often than not this audiovisual presentation serves as a filmmaker’s resume. It has more weight than the usual paper or text-based resume.

Music Video

A music video connects an artist and a listener, as well as connects a listener to the audience. In many ways, they are an important medium for contemporary pop culture and technology.

Rudra Kaushish

Owner and Producer
My real name is Gajendra Oza and as Gajendra was a very common name in this industry, I decided to change my name to Rudra Kaushish. You can read more on why I decided to pick that name in the news and media section of our website. I was born on 25th May 1973 in Jodhpur and in my young years, I served as a sub-inspector in the Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) for 12 years (1995 - 2007). A part of me always wanted to become an actor and I decided to roll the dice of my life and came to Mumbai. All the struggle I have been through taught me some valueable aspects of life and today, you might know me best for playing the role of Mohinder Singh Gill in the show Kulfi Kumar Bajewala. Recently I did a show called Dadi Amma… Dadi Amma Maan Jaao!, and have worked for shows like Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai, CID, Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashmah. My love for acting and film making led me to found a production house called Rekha G Films where I produced 6 short web films. I am open to collaborating with financers and other producers to help produce a content which makes an impact.

Chetan Sharma

Director
I was born in Uttar Pradesh and brought up in Mumbai. My love for content made me dive deep into the industry of direction and writing. I work on developing concepts for a film, scripting them to make it engaging and entertaining, and sometimes edit films by myself. I perceive the process of film making with a mindful and introspective approach to inject the films with a greater sense of purpose and meaning. You may know me best for directing the serial Tere Mere Sapne. I have directed the serials Kulfi Kumar Bajewaala, Balika Vadhu, Rishton Ka Saudagar: Baazigar, and Udaan. At Rekha G Films, I have wrote, directed, and edited all the 6 short web films and we are still working to grow our production house. Using the medium of film to express and explore the ideas that interest the audience, their personal struggles, and beliefs on a microcosmic level is my passion.
